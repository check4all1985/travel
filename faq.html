Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our travel packages and services

Booking & Payment

How do I book a travel package?

+

Booking is easy! Simply browse our packages, add your desired packages to the cart, and proceed to checkout. You can also call our customer service team at +44 20 7123 4567 for assistance with your booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.

Do I need to pay the full amount upfront?

+

For most packages, we require a 25% deposit to secure your booking, with the remaining balance due 30 days before departure. For last-minute bookings (less than 30 days), full payment is required upfront.

Travel & Accommodation

Are flights included in the packages?

+

Most of our packages include round-trip flights from major airports. However, some packages are available as "land-only" options if you prefer to arrange your own flights. Check the package details or contact us for clarification.

What type of accommodation can I expect?

+

We work with carefully selected hotels ranging from 3-star to luxury 5-star properties, depending on the package. All accommodations are personally vetted by our team to ensure quality and comfort.

Can I customize my travel package?

+

Yes! We offer customizable options for most packages. You can add extra nights, upgrade rooms, include additional tours, or modify itineraries. Contact our travel experts to discuss your specific requirements.

Cancellation & Refunds

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Our cancellation policy varies by package and timing:

  • More than 60 days before departure: Full refund minus £50 admin fee
  • 30-60 days before departure: 50% refund
  • Less than 30 days before departure: No refund

We recommend purchasing travel insurance for additional protection.

What if I need to change my travel dates?

+

Date changes are subject to availability and may incur additional fees. Contact us as soon as possible if you need to modify your travel dates. Changes made less than 30 days before departure may not be possible.

Travel Insurance & Safety

Is travel insurance included?

+

Basic travel insurance is included with all our packages, covering medical emergencies, trip cancellation, and lost luggage. You can upgrade to comprehensive coverage for additional protection.

How do you ensure traveler safety?

+

Traveler safety is our top priority. We work with reputable partners, provide 24/7 emergency support, monitor travel advisories, and ensure all accommodations and transportation meet strict safety standards.

General Questions

Do you offer group discounts?

+

Yes! We offer discounts for groups of 6 or more people. The discount amount varies based on group size and selected packages. Contact our group travel specialists for a custom quote.

Can I book for someone else as a gift?

+

Absolutely! We offer gift vouchers and can arrange bookings as surprises. Contact us with the recipient's details, and we'll help you create a memorable gift experience.

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend booking 2-3 months in advance for the best availability and prices. Peak season travel should be booked 4-6 months ahead. However, we also offer last-minute deals for flexible travelers.
